# About MyProvenance

Data provenance is the record of the origin, transformations and analyses a dataset goes through over time. In science, this history is what makes a result reproducible, lets you audit mistakes, correctly credit whoever produced each piece of data, and reuse work with confidence — it is the "R" for reusability in the FAIR principles.

MyProvenance is a simple, self-hosted tool for documenting that provenance following the international W3C PROV model, organized into three elements: Entities (the data), Activities (Creation, Transformation and Analysis) and Agents (who or what carried out each step). It records only the metadata of the process — who, when, how and with what — never the data itself: your files stay where they are, under your control.

As you record each step, MyProvenance automatically draws the lineage diagram and generates reports and exports ready to attach to a paper, repository or submission.
